Dungeon Runner VR

Unity 3D Project developed for the Google Cardboard and Android. The game is an infinite runner in which the player can rotate their heads to move sideways, jump by looking upwards and pressing the trigger button, and slide by looking downwards and pressing the trigger button. Although the project development period ran from June to December, I took approximately 2-3 months to build it. I learned more about procedural generation, how to use the Google Cardboard with Unity, effectively incorporating the Cardboard into the controls, and creating an effective yet simple User Interface to display information.
