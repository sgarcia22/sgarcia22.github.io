Stranded

Prototype UI made in Photoshop

Unity 3D Project developed for the Oculus Rift during the 2017 Summer Term; we had a team of five members. The user is placed in a town that has just been hit by a hurricane. The goal is to create a simulation of what to do in such a situation. I worked on creating the basic scene, constructing the water area and its script, and making the User Interface.
