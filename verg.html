Virtual Experiences for Dyslexia Awarness

Undergraduate Research Project being developed in Unity and later going to be exported to WebGL and Oculus Rift. The project aims to increase awareness of for students with dyslexia through a role-playing simulation that places the user in their shoes. It will have a functionality to switch from 'dyslexic' mode to 'non-dyslexic' mode to show users the stark contrast and hardships students and people with dyslexia may face. I am also serving as an assistant to a graduate researcher who is working on a virtual reality system to detect autism in young children.
