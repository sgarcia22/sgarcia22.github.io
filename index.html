Dr. Holo

Hololens AR App developed in Unity. The application focuses on optimizing patient care and reduce medical errors. The app's purpose is to provide Dr.’s and rotating medical staff “hands free” access to interact with patient information on inter-hospital secure medical database with potential to view Medical Imaging (MRI, Xray, Ultrasound) data in 3D to assist with treatment plan. I worked on initial UI design and implemented searching algorithm to retrieve patient information based on their Medical Record Number (MRN). I led four other members.

VR Self-Defense

There are countless reports of people being sexually and physically assaulted everyday. This application hopes to reduce the number of those occurrences by offering a self-defense virtual reality class to the public for free. I worked on game flow, detecting sphere collision and checking if the player violated any rules or took too long to hit the spheres. I also helped set up U.I., initial scenes, music, Oculus Touch controls, and haptics. The project was developed in Unity3D in under 36 hours for the Swamphacks 2018 Hackathon and uses the Oculus Rift and Touch controllers.

Dungeon Runner VR

Unity 3D Project developed for the Google Cardboard and Android. The game is an infinite runner in which the player can rotate their heads to move sideways, jump by looking upwards and pressing the trigger button, and slide by looking downwards and pressing the trigger button. Although the project development period ran from June to December, I took approximately 2-3 months to build it. I learned more about procedural generation, how to use the Google Cardboard with Unity, effectively incorporating the Cardboard into the controls, and creating an effective yet simple User Interface to display information.

There is no Floor

Stranded

Unity 3D Project developed for the Oculus Rift during the 2017 Summer Term; we had a team of five members. The user is placed in a town that has just been hit by a hurricane. The goal is to create a simulation of what to do in such a situation. I worked on creating the basic scene, constructing the water area and its script, and making the User Interface.
